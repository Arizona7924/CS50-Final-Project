Welcome!

I'm Mia, a portrait photographer from Virinia Beach with a special focus on capturing the beauty of weddings. My journey into photography began nearly three years ago when I borrowed a camera at college and quickly fell in love with the art. Since then, I've been developing my skill, learning from professionals and experimenting on my own.

 

What I love most about photography is the joy it brings to people around me. Photography is such a rewarding experience as it allows me to use my passion to bless others by creating images that they love. My goal is to ensure that every couple has stunning memories they can cherish forever. I look forward to the opportunity to capture your special moments!
